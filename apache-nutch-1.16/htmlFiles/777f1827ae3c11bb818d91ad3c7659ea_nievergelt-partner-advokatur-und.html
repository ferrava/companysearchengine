ABOUT US

Nievergelt & Partner is one of the leading law firms and notary offices in Graubunden. For over 20 years, the law firm has been successfully advising individuals, companies, communities and corporations. Nievergelt & Partner lawyers work at two locations in the Engadin: Samedan and St. Moritz.

We provide comprehensive counselling to our clients in legal matters and endeavour to provide solution and goal-oriented recommendations.

Our lawyers work in multiple disciplines and languages, providing advice and information in German, English, Italian and Romansh.
