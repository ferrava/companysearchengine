Les Trois Rois

Rich in history,
young at heart.

Welcome to our hotel

The Grand Hotel Les Trois Rois lies at the very heart of the city of Basel. At the exact spot where, in the Middle Ages, the ships docked at the salt tower in order to transport that much sought-after product down the Rhine and out into the world. Discover Basel, the tri-border area and the charm of our region. Allow yourself to be pampered – to make your stay in our small cosmopolitan city unforgettable.

This is what we stand for

  • 01 A great hotel,
    illustrious history,
    riveting stories

    With over 300 years of history, our Grand Hotel has one of the richest traditions of all the hotels in Europe. It has housed royalty, rock legends and poets.

  • 02 Culinary delights
    for all the senses

    Experience the only Grand Hotel in Switzerland with a
    restaurant with 3 Michelin stars & 19 Gault Millau points.

                  

  • 03 Location on the banks
    of the Rhine

    The Grand Hotel Les Trois Rois is located in the heart of the historic Old City of Basel with breathtaking views of the Rhine. The trade fair, museums and pedestrian shopping area can all be easily reached on foot.

  • 04 Feeling at home –
    feeling comfortable

    101 rooms and suites are at the disposal of our guests. They meet every requirement and combine history with the most modern comfort.

  • 05 Young at heart
    Projects

    Learn all about "The Bentley Project" a "Rich in history, young at heart" project.
