Your place to stay in Ascona

The Jazz Hotel has been renovated and opened in 2018 and is the best choice for a peaceful and carefree stay in Ascona. The rooms are comfortable and the staff is very friendly. The rich breakfast is served in the hall on the first floor or on the outdoor terrace. Some useful information:

free mineral water and coffee, 24/24 internet connection, non-smoking hotel, pets are not allowed, reception is served until 20.00, after which you can check in electronically at the building entrance, check-out by 11.00 am, internal parking available.
