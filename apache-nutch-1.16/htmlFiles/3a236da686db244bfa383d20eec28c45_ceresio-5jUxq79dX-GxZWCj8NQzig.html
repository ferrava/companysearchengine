Description and special features

Located in Lugano square for over 40 years, Hotel Ceresio is your ideal hotel when exploring the beautiful city of Lugano. Just a few steps from the historical centre, the main monuments and centres of interest such as the Congress Hall and the Swiss-Italian University, the hotel will be the ideal point of reference during your stay.

The qualified staff will be happy to greet you in a family, yet professional atmosphere, with a wide range of amenities such as private parking, free wireless Internet connection, direct dial telephone and a restaurant ready to coddle you with a taste of Ticino cuisine.

The cozy rooms, conceived in a nice and pleasant setting, will be ready to welcome you with all essentials conveniently at your service.

Our experience, gained through catering to a large number of multinational clients, will always be able to offer a commercial space suitable for every business need.

Our restaurant is always open at lunchtime (i.e 12.00 – 14.00) from Monday to Friday and for dinner under reservation (min. 10 pp).

We are proud to offer traditional, genuine and healthy Ticino cuisine, attentively served by a friendly staff and at fair prices. With a rich menu available to all clients, our goal is to pamper our guests in a special manner, spoiling them with delicious dishes of typical Ticino cuisine.

Among all, one cannot miss an enriched breakfast to start off your day  in the best way. Thanks to our excellent location and easy parking facilities, you may enjoy your day exploring the city of Lugano at leisure.

Upon request we offer business lunch or dinner for workshops organised at our facilities.
