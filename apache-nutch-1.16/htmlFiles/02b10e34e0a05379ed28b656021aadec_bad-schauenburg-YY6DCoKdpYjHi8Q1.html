The charming oasis in the Basel region

Untouched nature, seasonal culinary delights and warm hospitality: At Bad Schauenburg you will find a charming oasis that awakens all senses. Nestled in one of the most charming areas of the Basel region, restaurants, hotel and seminar rooms invite you to a truly revitalizing time-out.

Leave the hectic city behind you and enjoy fresh culinary ideas on our sun terrace. Spend inspiring seminars and meetings overlooking the beautiful park. Rest after hiking at one of the cozy garden tables. Or stay for a few romantic nights at the legendary spa hotel of earlier centuries.

Welcome to Bad Schauenburg. Awaken your senses.
