Description and special features

The Hotel-Garni MURALTO is the result of the complete restoration of the former Hotel Carmine, located in Via Sempione 10 in Locarno-Muralto.
The building, constructed in the 1960s, is situated in a very sunny, easily accessible central position. In 2007 it was acquired by the Gian-Luigi and Laura Varini family who decided to completely renovate it in order to obtain a modern and comfortable structure that satisfies current needs.
