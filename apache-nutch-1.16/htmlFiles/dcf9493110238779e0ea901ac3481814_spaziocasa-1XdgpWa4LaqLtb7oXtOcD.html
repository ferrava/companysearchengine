ABOUT US

SpazioCasa is a modern company that deals with kitchens and home furnishings.

The excellent value for money of our products is one of our strengths. With SpazioCasa you will find the ideal partner to furnish your home with quality furnishings and even with turnkey solutions.
We are a company that is attentive to customer needs and its complete satisfaction.
We take care of the design and production of kitchens and custom-made furniture of high quality, devoting great attention to details from design to final installation.
We work with passion and transparency in contact with the customer to put him in the condition of having the best product at a competitive price.
We mainly address private customers but we also offer advice to professionals such as architects and construction companies by offering interesting conditions. Call us for a free quote.
