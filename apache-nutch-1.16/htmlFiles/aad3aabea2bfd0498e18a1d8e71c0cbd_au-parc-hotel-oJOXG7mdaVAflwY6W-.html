Au Parc Hotel Fribourg

A few minutes from the city center, our modern family hotel is committed to the quality of its service.
The warm welcome and our knowledge are our customers' satisfaction and offers the ideal setting for meetings.
When you arrive at the Au Parc Hotel Fribourg, you will get seduced by an establishment where everything was designed for your comfort.
Welcome home!

OUR CONFERENCE ROOMS
We have 12 conference and flexible banquet rooms with modern technical development.
They are soundproofed, woody, have variable brightness and natural day light.
Turquoise and Topaz are air conditioned.
For your seminar from 10 to 150 people, we will put a point to meet your demands.
The success of your seminars measures themselves to their impact. We offer you a clear and effective organization.

HOTEL
For your stay, we have 66 rooms, 2 junior suites, 2 apartment - studio and 1 apparthôtel - 2,5 rooms.

RESTAURANTS
Our brasserie La Terrasse offers a refined and creative menu, mainly based on seasonal products.
Our Thai La Coupole specializes in Thai cuisine and certified for authenticity and flavors.

PARKINGS 110 parking spaces available.
