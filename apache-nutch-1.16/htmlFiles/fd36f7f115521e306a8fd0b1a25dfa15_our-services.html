local.ch - search, find, book

Number one for restaurant bookings in Switzerland

The platform

local.ch is the number one search directory and the leading booking platform for restaurants in Switzerland. On local.ch, you can quickly search for and easily find information, reserve tables efficiently and make bookings online at any time.


Thanks to local.ch, you always have the information you need at hand and you can make bookings when you have time. local.ch is also available as an app and a printed directory (localGuide).

SMEs available to book

local.ch app – more than 3.5 million downloads

In addition to attractive booking options and the directory, you can take advantage of other helpful functions with the local.ch app, such as:


  • Caller ID: You can always see who has tried to contact you, even if their number is not in your contact list. 
  • Telemarketing blocker: Use the app to automatically block recognised and verified telemarketers.
  • Vicinity search: Find useful places nearby, such as ATMs, petrol stations, car parks, public toilets or WiFi hotspots, incl. map entries.
  • Special offers: Benefit from special offers from various businesses.

localGuide – Switzerland’s official telephone book

Our localGuide is the most widely used printed telephone directory in Switzerland. It is still extremely popular, even alongside the electronic directories local.ch and search.ch.


It consists of the well-known Yellow Pages, the telephone directory containing telephone numbers and addresses of businesses and private individuals, as well as an editorial which offers an interesting introduction with numerous articles of an advisory nature. Here you will find important and helpful information about your region or town.

Order your localGuide

With regard to the localGuide, local.ch is mindful of making a sustainable contribution to environmental protection by only sending addressed telephone directories to households and maintaining its address database, including cancellations. Our aim is to only send the localGuide to households and people who actually use it.
