PURE URBAN RETREAT

BEAUTY & WELLNESS WITH THE POWER OF NATURE

Pure was launched in 2007 with the aim to create a holistic urban retreat offering a wide selection of beauty and wellness treatments all under one roof. Our multi-cultural, community atmosphere honors local traditions and introduces guests to the latest products and treatments in hair, beauty and wellness. We partner with brands that support and strengthen the Pure philosophy. We specialize in brands that are ethically-sourced, organic in nature, environmentally conscious, and results-oriented to meet the lifestyle needs of our guests.
