Hotel Gasthof zum Ochsen

Enjoyment of meat with a long tradition
For over 300 years the Ochsen has stood for the enjoyment of best quality meat in the hospitable surroundings of the Gasthaus zum Ochsen in Arlesheim near Basel. We can look back with pleasure and pride at over 100 years of our family history.

And so it is today – Christoph and Barbara Jenzer maintains a delicate balancing act between hotel, butcher’s shop and delicatessen with dedication and enthusiasm.  They are energetically supported by hotel manager Lukas Dobler, F&B manager Sibylle Böhler and an enthusiastic and motivated team.
The proximity to Basel, the museum city on the trinational border with the open countryside on its doorstep, make Arlesheim an endearing and attractive place.
