Description and special features

The NH Fribourg hotel is in the Grand-Places area of Fribourg in Switzerland, just minutes from the train station. A pretty park is close by and it’s an easy walk to major tourist attractions, shops and restaurants.

  • in the heart of the town and close to the station
  • 15-minute walk to Saint Nicholas Cathedral, for magnificent stained windows and panoramic views
  • less than half-an-hour to the Old Town’s cozy cafés, churches and Gothic architecture

Our 122 rooms give you plenty of options, like single rooms for lone travelers and connecting rooms for families. Several come with fantastic mountain and river views.

  • view of the pre-Alps from many rooms
  • room service and free Wi-Fi
  • Junior suite with Jacuzzi and Nespresso machine

We’ve recently renovated our events rooms – the biggest takes 800 people. And we have a lovely terrace that’s popular for al fresco drinks and dining.

  • banquet rooms renovated in 2013
  • terrace restaurant
  • cozy bar
  • If you're staying with us over the weekend take advantage of "Lazy Sunday", and checkout late (before 5pm) free of charge.
