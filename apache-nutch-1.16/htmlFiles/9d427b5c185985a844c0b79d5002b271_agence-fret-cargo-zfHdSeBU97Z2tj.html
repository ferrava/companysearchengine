Description and special features

For over 30 years, we have been transporting goods, luggage and personal belongings around the world. Our team will offer you personalized quick and easy solution for your shipment, at the best price. Our price may surprise you!

Agence Fret Cargo forward a wide range of shipments, which includes personal effects, diplomatic putch, pets, precious artworks and race cars. For shipments with the tightest transport deadlines, we also offer express services.

Land, water or air transportation: we safely send whatever you want to more than 200 countries around the world.
