HITrental Apartments - furnished, individual and comfortable

The HITrental AG offers comfortable and unique holiday rentals and business apartments in different cities of Switzerland. Our apartments in Cham, Lucerne, Zug and Zurich provide modern furnishing, fully equipped kitchens, free wifi and a great price-value-ratio. In a top location not far from public transport and all the attractions, you can enjoy your stay with us to the fullest.

Our apartments can be rented for short stays from 3 nights or for longer stays. Due to the flexibility of the booking period, we are an ideal alternative to the standardized hotels.

Our apartments shine through its individuality. This is also represented by the names of the apartments. For example, our HITrental Stauffacher Apartments, that are located near the football stadium in Zurich, are named after legendary and famous players of the FC Zürich

About us - our philosophy

The HITrental AG was founded in 2011 by the Hess family. Since then, the family business has grown steadily and continues to expand to this day. We currently offer our clients more than 200 furnished and serviced apartments.

Despite the steady growth, family Hess always emphasized the importance of maintaining family flair and an uncomplicated atmosphere in the company. This is one of the reasons why all HITrental employees always use their first names for internal as well as external communication. This uncomplicated and family type of behavior is directly transmitted to our guests during the booking process. It is a big task to find an optimal and individual solution for every customer. As a result we are only satisfied if the customer is.

For further information about our various holiday rentals and business apartments, please visit our website.
