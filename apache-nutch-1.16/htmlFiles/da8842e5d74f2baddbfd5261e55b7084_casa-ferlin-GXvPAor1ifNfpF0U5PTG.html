Description and special features

Ristorante Casa Ferlin is an Italian restaurant in Zurich. Our traditional Italian restaurant "Casa Ferlin" is close to Stampfenbachplatz. Guests enjoy a warm, traditional and Venetian / Italian ambience. For our homemade ravioli, the veal fillet of lemon sauce and the creamy mousse au chocolat, we are well known. Best cuisine since 1907 The chef Alessandro Rovito puts a lot of emphasis on fresh produce. For 15 years he spoils the guests with Italian, Mediterranean, seasonal, Swiss and international dishes. Managing Director Franz Ferlin and the team maintain a familiar and personal approach to new customers and numerous regular guests.
